PW Keeper

This is a really stupid approach to having long pseudo random passwords without having to remember them. This is not secure and I do not recommend using this.

The password is removed from screen after 1 minute. You can clear all fields and password output by pressing escape on your keyboard or clicking the "Clear everything" button.
